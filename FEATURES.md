# PaySplit — Features

PaySplit helps friends and groups **split expenses without the back-and-forth**. Add shared costs, see who owes what, and settle up with clarity.

## Split with confidence

- **Groups for every part of life**: Trips, roommates, dinners, projects — create a group and keep everything organized.
- **Smart group setup**: Add a group description, pick members right away, and get an icon suggestion based on the group name.
- **Flexible splitting**: Split **equally**, enter **exact amounts**, or split by **percent**.
- **Multi-currency friendly**: Track expenses in different currencies without forcing conversions.
- **Keep things tidy**: Archive groups when they’re done, and bring them back anytime.

## Stay on top of who owes what

- **Clear balances**: Instantly see whether you **owe**, you’re **owed**, or you’re **all settled**.
- **Member-by-member breakdown**: See balances per person inside a group, including multi-currency balances when needed.
- **Settle up in seconds**: Record a payment, choose a payment method, and add a note so everyone stays aligned.

## Faster expense entry

- **All the details you need**: Add a description, amount, currency, who paid, and who’s included.
- **Receipt support**: Attach a receipt image to keep details handy.
- **Categories that keep things tidy**: Label expenses (food, travel, shopping, and more) so your history is easy to browse.
- **Expense details at a glance**: Open any expense to see the split across members.
- **Edit or delete**: Fix mistakes fast — update an expense, or delete it if it shouldn’t be there.

## Bring friends in easily

- **Invite to a group**: Add friends who use the app, or generate invite links you can share for contacts.
- **Built-in sharing**: Send invites and reminders using your phone’s share sheet (Messages, WhatsApp, email, and more).
- **Friends view**: See shared balances with people you split with across groups.

## Helpful insights

- **Activity feed**: Review recent expenses and settlements at a glance, with filters to find what matters.
- **Group timeline**: Expenses are organized over time so you can quickly revisit what happened.
- **Spending summary**: See where money went by category and share a summary when needed.

## Built for everyday use

- **Easy sign-in**: Sign in with email/password or continue with Google.
- **Smooth onboarding**: Add your phone number and (optionally) connect contacts to find friends faster.
- **Your preferences**: Choose your default currency, update your phone number, and switch app language.
- **Profile at a glance**: See your name and email in one place, and sign out anytime.
- **AI helper (optional)**: Ask for quick help and jump straight to common actions like adding an expense or checking balances.
- **Dark mode**: Comfortable to use day or night.
- **Multiple languages**: Designed to work across many locales.
